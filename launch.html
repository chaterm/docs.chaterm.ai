Hi Product Hunt 👋

I'm Walter, founder of the open-source project Chaterm, and an SRE with over 10 years of experience.

In my past roles, our team managed over ten thousands of servers, dozens of Kubernetes clusters, and hundreds of databases. One of the biggest challenges we constantly faced was helping junior engineers quickly become productive in such complex operational environments.

That’s why we built Chaterm — an AI-native terminal designed specifically for infrastructure and cloud resource management, it can bridge the gap between infrastructure intent and execution.

Instead of memorizing complex CLI flags or digging through documentation, you simply describe your goal in natural language. Chaterm understands your unique system topology to autonomously plan and execute workflows—handling everything from multi-cluster deployments and anomaly diagnosis to automatic rollbacks.

Our core philosophy:

● From Commands to Execution: Let the AI handle the "heavy lifting" of planning and running complex tasks.

● Reusable Agent Skills: Capture your team's senior expertise into automated skills that anyone can trigger safely.

● SRE Superpowers for Everyone: We're lowering the barrier to entry, giving every developer the execution power of a veteran SRE.

We’re excited to share Chaterm with the PH community and would truly appreciate your feedback. We’ll be here all day answering any questions and discuss ideas.
